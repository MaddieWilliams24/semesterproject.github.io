People in Austrailia are known for their love of the outdoors, laid-back approach to life, and weekend cafe culture. As most of Austrailia's major cities are found along the coastline, many people go to the beach to swim or to the park for lunch. Due to the huge size of the country, Austrailia has several different climate zones. The northern section of Austrailia has a more tropical influenced climate, hot and humid in the summer, and quite warm in the winter, while the southern parts are cooler with mild summers and cool, sometimes rainy winters. An interesting aspect of the country is it's wildlife: kangaroos, koalas, wallabies, wombats, and even platypuses call this country their home. In general, life in Austrailia mostly about being outside. The weather is often very sunny and warm, perfect for being out with friends or finding a cool place outdoors to relax.
